Privacy

What we collect

Email addresses submitted on the waitlist form, and standard website analytics (page views, approximate location, device type). IP addresses are anonymized before processing.

How we use it

Your email is stored so we can notify you when coronin launches. We don't send newsletters, promotions, or unrelated messages. We don't sell or rent email addresses. Analytics are used in aggregate to understand traffic.

Third parties

We use reputable third-party services for email delivery and website analytics. They process data on our behalf under their own privacy commitments.

Your rights

Request deletion of your email, a copy of your data, or unsubscribe at any time by emailing hello@coronin.ai. EU, UK, and California residents have additional rights under GDPR and CCPA; to exercise them, contact us at the same address.

Changes

If we meaningfully change our practices, we'll update this page.
